Step by step guide to use Selene Networth Calculator

Welcome, Follow this guide to start using Selene Networth Calculator



STEP 1


From the homepage click the Login button



STEP 2


Click Signup




STEP 3


Fill in your correct details and click submit




STEP 4


Use the email and password you used to register to login




STEP 5


In the dashboard click on Networth Calculator




STEP 6


Make sure you fill all the field, the red is your worth and the blue is liabilites




STEP 7


Click Calculate to get your Networth
